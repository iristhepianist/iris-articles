Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 6, 2025

Overview

Œª Youth Journalism ("we", "our", or "us") operates this website and the Lambda Articles mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our services.

Information Collection and Use

Website

Our website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We collect minimal information:

  • No cookies - We don't use tracking cookies or analytics
  • LocalStorage - Only to remember your dark mode preference and newsletter subscription (stored locally in your browser)
  • Newsletter subscriptions - If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address and optional name are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This data is not transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly export it
  • No user accounts - We don't require registration or login

Mobile App

The Lambda Articles mobile app collects minimal data:

  • No personal information - We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information
  • No account required - The app does not require user registration or login
  • Cached articles - Articles are cached locally on your device for offline reading. This data never leaves your device
  • No analytics or tracking - We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services

Network Requests

The mobile app makes network requests to:

  • https://iristhepianist.github.io/iris-articles/articles.json - to fetch article content

These requests do not include any personal information.

Data Storage

  • Website: Dark mode preference and newsletter subscriptions are stored in your browser's localStorage. No data is sent to our servers automatically
  • Newsletter: Email addresses and optional names are stored locally in your browser only. Editors can export subscriber lists manually to send newsletters via third-party email services
  • Mobile App: All cached data is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. We do not sync or backup your data to any cloud service

GitHub Pages Hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect technical information such as IP addresses for security and performance purposes. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for more information.

Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Third-Party Links

Our articles may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.

Data Deletion

Website

To clear your dark mode preference and newsletter subscription:

  1. Open your browser's developer tools (F12)
  2. Go to the "Application" or "Storage" tab
  3. Find "Local Storage" and delete the items for this site (look for "darkMode" and "lambda_newsletter_subscribers")
  4. Or simply use your browser's "Clear browsing data" feature
  5. You can also unsubscribe by clicking the unsubscribe link in any newsletter email

Mobile App

To delete all cached data:

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Go to Apps ‚Üí Lambda (or "lambda")
  3. Tap "Storage"
  4. Tap "Clear Data" or "Clear Storage"
