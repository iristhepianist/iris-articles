# Privacy Policy for Lambda Articles Android App

**Last updated:** December 5, 2025

## Overview

Lambda Articles ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Lambda Articles mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data We Collect

The Lambda Articles app collects minimal data:

- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information.
- **No Account Required**: The app does not require user registration or login.
- **Cached Articles**: Articles are cached locally on your device for offline reading using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

### Network Requests

The app makes network requests to:
- `https://iristhepianist.github.io/iris-articles/articles.json` - to fetch article content

These requests do not include any personal information.

### Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage Only**: All cached data is stored locally on your device
- **No Cloud Sync**: We do not sync or backup your data to any cloud service
- **No User Accounts**: We do not maintain user accounts or store user data on our servers

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is minimal security risk. Cached articles are stored using standard Android storage mechanisms.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
